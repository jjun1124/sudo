Privacy Policy

1. No Data Collection Policy

EasyLife (sudo!404), including 'Fill My House with Water', prioritizes user privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data to external servers.

2. Permissions & Usage

We request only the minimum permissions necessary for core features:

  • Storage: Used only to save game scores and "sudo_note" content locally on your device.
  • ActiveTab: Used only when you explicitly run tools like dark mode or unlocking features on a specific tab.

3. Third-Party Disclosure

Since no data is collected or transmitted, we have no data to share with third parties. Your data remains strictly on your local device.

Compliance: Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA, Korea), Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA, USA).
