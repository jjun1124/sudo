Privacy Policy

1. No Data Collection

sudo!404 (including 'Our Home Water Fill Game') values your privacy. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data to any external servers.

2. Permissions & Usage

We only request permissions essential for core functionality:

  • Storage: To save your local game scores and "sudo_note" content on your own computer.
  • ActiveTab: Only used when you explicitly trigger tools (like Dark Mode or Unlocker) on a specific tab.

3. Third-Party Disclosure

Since no data is ever collected or sent to us, we have no data to share with third parties. Your data remains strictly on your local machine.

Compliance: PIPA (South Korea), COPPA (USA) Standards.

Core Features

Web Unlocker

Freely select and copy content even on websites with copy protection or right-click restrictions. Neutralizes event blocking with stopPropagation.
